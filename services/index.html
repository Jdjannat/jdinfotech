Engagement Models

Choose the model that best fits your project needs and business goals.

Fixed Price Model

Perfect for well-defined projects with clear scope and deliverables. Ideal when both parties have a detailed understanding of requirements and the timeline is established before project kickoff.

Predictable costsClear timelineWell-defined scope

Time & Material Model

Best for projects with evolving requirements and ambiguous scope. Resources are provided and billed based on time spent. Offers flexibility to adapt and scale as needs become clearer.

Flexible scopeHourly billingAdaptive approach

A dedicated team of specialists working exclusively for your project over a fixed period. Acts as an extension of your internal team, ideal for ongoing development and continuous improvement.
